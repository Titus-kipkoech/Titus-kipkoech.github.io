Welcome

This is the official online portal for the Students Organization of Kibabii University (SOKU). Here, you will find announcements, event details, leadership information, and resources for all students.

Our mission is to represent, support, and enhance the student experience at Kibabii University.

Key Activities

  • Student Representation: Advocating for student needs and welfare.
  • Events & Activities: Organizing cultural, academic, and social events.
  • Information Hub: Centralizing important announcements and university updates.
  • Community Building: Fostering a strong, inclusive student community.
